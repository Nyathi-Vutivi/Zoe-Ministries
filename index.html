About Us

Zoe Christian International Ministries is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ and making disciples of all nations.

Our mission is to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ through worship, fellowship, and service. We believe in the power of God's word to transform lives and communities.

Founded on the principles of faith, hope, and love, we strive to be a beacon of light in our community and beyond.

Our Services
